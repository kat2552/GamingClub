East Los Angeles Gaming Club

Our Gaming Club mission is to teach our club members the skills set of collaboration, leadership, and to build upon business strategies in the gaming commerce industry.
The gaming club is broken into the PC E-Sports gaming team and our Virtual Reality team! The Gaming Club meets every Tuesday at 5pm weekly. We are part of the district grant.
We are one of the nine schools participating in competitive eports.
We welcome ALL Players!
You could be a long time gamer, console gamers, to a first time PC gamers.
Even those who've never played video games before!
We have an excellent coach and team mebers who are more than happy to train you along your gaming journey!
